Warrior Cats Books — Complete Study Guide and Summaries

The Warriors Atlas is a free, SparkNotes-style study guide to every Warrior Cats book by Erin Hunter. For each novel it provides a full plot summary, themes, motifs, critical analysis, character analysis, study questions, memorable quotes, a relationship map of the cats and Clans, the book's placement in the saga's timeline, and comparisons to other famous books. The guide covers all eight arcs — The Prophecies Begin, The New Prophecy, Power of Three, Omen of the Stars, Dawn of the Clans, A Vision of Shadows, The Broken Code, and A Starless Clan — as well as the Super Editions such as Bluestar's Prophecy, Firestar's Quest, Yellowfang's Secret, and Crookedstar's Promise. Explore the Clans — ThunderClan, ShadowClan, RiverClan, WindClan, SkyClan, and StarClan — on an interactive 3D map.
